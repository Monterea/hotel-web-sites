Stay in the heart of Tunis
You're eligible for a Genius discount at El Mouradi Hotel Africa Tunis! To save at this property, all you have to do is sign in. Situated in Tunis, on the famous Avenue Habib Bourguiba, Hotel Africa Tunis is a 15-minute drive from Tunis-Carthage Airport. It offers soundproofed and air-conditioned rooms with panoramic views over the city. The Hotel Africa Tunis’ restaurant, Kilimandjaro, serves Mediterranean cuisine and guests are invited to enjoy at the Lobby bar. There is also a Brasserie situated on the terrace, which overlooks the Avenue de Tunis. The Africa has a 24-hour reception and provides free newspapers, currency exchange and a car rental service. Free private parking is available on site and guests can discover the local medina and souks of Tunis. The Lake of Tunis is just 5 km away. Couples particularly like the location — they rated it 8.9 for a two-person trip. El Mouradi Hotel Africa Tunis has been welcoming Booking.com guests since 22 Jul 2009.
Children of any age are welcome. Children aged 6 years and above are considered adults at this property. Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay. There is no capacity for cots at this property. The maximum number of extra beds allowed depends on the room you choose. Please check the maximum capacity for the room you selected. All cots and extra beds are subject to availability.
